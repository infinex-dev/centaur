Fund Infinex from your bank

You can now fund Infinex directly from a bank account. Choose Bank transfer in the deposit dialog, accept the terms, and verify your identity. Infinex then issues a permanent US account number and routing number in your name.

How it works

Send USD to that account by ACH push or wire, the same way you move money to any account. The funds convert to USDC automatically and arrive on-chain at the destination wallet address you specify. Verify once, and the account stays yours to reuse for every deposit after.

Before your first transfer

Bank transfer runs on Bridge.xyz. You complete KYC identity verification through the Bridge.xyz hosted flow and accept Bridge.xyz Terms of Service before your virtual account is created: details, accept terms, verify identity.
