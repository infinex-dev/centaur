3-shell Laban viz — operator's architecture (2026-05-18)

Inner core (4 small nodes): Aspects — Enclosing (Weight-led) / Penetrating (Space-led) / Radiating (Flow-led) / Circumscribing (Time-led). Each connects to its 3 legal Inner Attitudes.
Middle ring (6 hexagon nodes): Inner Attitudes — Stable / Near / Adream (baselines, solid stroke) + Awake / Mobile / Remote (action attitudes, dashed stroke). Mirodan canonical colors.
Outer ring (24 dots): Tempi — 4 per Inner Attitude, fanning radially outward. Dot size = sample count.

Note: the source classifier was missing adream and remote from the inner_attitude enum, and missing enclosing and radiating from the aspect enum. So those nodes/sectors will read as empty in this smoke. Tempi populated where Sonnet emitted a recognized tempo name. Re-classify with corrected enum to see full distribution.
