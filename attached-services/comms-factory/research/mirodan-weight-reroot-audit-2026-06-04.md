# Mirodan Weight grounding — re-root audit (2026-06-04)

Audit of the 2026-06-04 Weight calibration (`scripts/classify-corpus.ts` "Reading Weight" + `research/mirodan-weight-motor-grounding-2026-06-04.md`) against the primary source (`third_party/mirodan/ch1,ch2`).

## VERDICT: OVER-NARROWED

The slogan **"Weight is muscular force / exertion against resistance — nothing else"** drops the half of the definition the source insists on: Weight is the visible trace of **Intending** = "intentions, desires, needs, the 'will-power' to act" (Ch1 p.281, 293). Ch1 p.312 explicitly forbids reading the physical-force layer in isolation from its Intending root.

The **operational discriminator** ("epistemic certainty / unhedged finality is not by itself Weight; it's Space-Direct + Awake-certain") is FAITHFUL. The error is the framing, not the gate.

Two concrete defects:
1. **"force, nothing else"** amputates Weight = asserted **will/intention** (p.280–281, 293; Stable = "strong will… bold resolve" p.487, 494). Asserted will ("we will take it down," "this stops," "I refuse") is legitimate Strong Weight with zero literal muscle.
2. **"certain is Weightless by definition / ZERO Weight"** overstates p.474/481. Awake has no Weight in its *constitutive* pair (Space+Time) — Weight is **latent, not absent**. The "certain" tempo's own Subconscious Motif is **Pressing**, a Strong-Weight action (p.481). "Weight non-constitutive" ≠ "Weightless as proof."

## The corrected axis
The real discriminator is **volitional will (Intending → Weight) vs. epistemic settledness (Attending/Deciding → Space+Time)** — NOT "force vs. confidence."

- Confidence about a **fact** ("this is true / live / final") → Awake **certain** (Space+Time). Not Weight.
- Confidence as an act of **will** ("we will," "I refuse," "this ends now") → Stable **commanding** (Weight+Space). Weight.

## Why this caused the 0%-commanding symptom
The over-narrowed rule told the classifier to require *physical force* and treat all confidence as non-Weight. So every asserted-will line (legitimately commanding under Infinex's Stable placement) got dumped into "certain." On human Infinex copy commanding fell 19% → 0% and certain rose 3% → 33%. That's the amputation, visible in the numbers.

## Does it overturn the Sonnet-monotone finding?
No. Human copy showed 14–18 distinct tempi even under the over-narrowed rule (it breathes regardless). The Sonnet-42 collapsed to 1 tempo. The core finding (judge honest, generator monotone) is robust. BUT the commanding/certain *split* and the exact label on the Sonnet-42 were produced by the over-narrowed rule and must be re-derived under the corrected rule before (a) trusting the label, (b) porting anything to `src/actor-memory.ts` production Director.

## CORRECTED WEIGHT RULE (drop-in for classify-corpus.ts "Reading Weight")

> **Reading Weight (LOAD-BEARING).** Weight is the visible trace of **Intending** — will, intention, desire, the push to impose oneself on circumstances (Ch1 p.281, 293) — expressed physically as muscular force / counter-pressure against gravity (Ch1 p.292, 316). Read it from **will + exertion together**, never a physical-force checklist alone (p.312: the factor has no meaning split from its Intending root).
> **Strong-Weight evidence** = asserted will / resolve / desire pushing against resistance ("we will take it down," "this stops," "I refuse"), OR literal force / mass / heavy landing. **Light** = buoyancy, fine touch, will withheld.
> **Epistemic certainty is NOT Weight.** "This is simply true / final / unhedged" is Space-**Direct** + Awake-**certain** (Space+Time) — settled *awareness*, not asserted *will* (Ch1 p.298, 321; Ch2 p.481). Awake carries no Weight in its constitutive pair (p.474) — Weight is **latent, not absent** (the "certain" motor is Pressing, a Strong action, p.481); never cite certainty as "proof of zero Weight."
> **Commanding vs certain test:** both are Direct. Does the line **exert will / impose a resolve** (→ commanding, Stable, Weight+Space, Punching/Pressing, p.494) or **register a settled truth/awareness** (→ certain, Awake, Space+Time, p.481)? Volitional pressure, not mere confidence, is the Weight signal.

Example 2 (`classify-corpus.ts` ~L460–465) outcome stays correct (the HL-spot line registers settled fact = Awake certain, no will against resistance) but its justification "Awake certain is Weightless… ZERO Weight" must soften to "Weight is latent/non-constitutive in Awake — this registers settled awareness, not asserted will."
