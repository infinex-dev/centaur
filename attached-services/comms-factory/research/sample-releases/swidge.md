## Voice notes
Swidge is the easiest product to be wry about because the *name* is already in on the joke ("aka swidge" — Infinex named it like a meme and shipped it like infrastructure). Lean into the named-the-thing confidence; never explain the joke. The risk is sounding cute about money movement — keep the data card sober (real route, real saved fee), let the caption carry the personality. Never disparage a specific bridge by name; the 25 providers are *partners*, not punchlines.
