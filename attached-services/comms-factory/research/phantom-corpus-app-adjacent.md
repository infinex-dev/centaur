## Capture caveats

- `help.phantom.com` is on Zendesk behind Cloudflare; WebFetch and curl both received HTTP 403. Article text above is from Google's indexed snippets — verbatim where shown in quotes, but coverage is shallow (1-3 sentences per article, never the full body). For deeper help-center capture, an authenticated browser session or scraper with valid Cloudflare cookies is required.
- Google Play full description recovered from snippet was matched to the iOS body for cross-verification; the bullet list differs (Google Play copy is the older Solana+Ethereum-era variant — A025/A026 mention NFTs and "Solana and Ethereum dapps"; iOS copy is updated, drops NFTs as headline, generalizes to "Crypto" + "your favorite sites").
- No screenshot-caption text was retrievable from either store (App Store renders screenshots as images; WebFetch can't OCR them).
