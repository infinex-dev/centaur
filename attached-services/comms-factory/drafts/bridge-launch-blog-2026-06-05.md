# Fund Infinex straight from your bank

Your bank account and your Infinex wallet used to be two separate places, with an exchange in the middle. Bank transfer closes the gap: send dollars from your bank, and they arrive in your Infinex wallet as USDC.

Bank transfer is a new deposit method inside Infinex, built with Bridge.xyz, the stablecoin-infrastructure company Stripe acquired. Here is exactly how it works, what to expect, and what it doesn't do yet.

## How to set it up

In Infinex, open **Deposit**, then choose **Bank transfer** under "Other ways to deposit." Accept the terms and **verify your identity**. This is a one-time check, handled by Bridge; it usually takes a few minutes, and up to one business day. Once you're approved, pick **USD bank transfer**, and you're issued a permanent US account number and routing number in your name.

## How to deposit

Send US dollars to that account by **ACH or wire**, the same way you move money to any account. The funds convert to USDC automatically and arrive on-chain in your main Infinex wallet on Base. You don't pick a destination and you don't touch the conversion; the dollars land as USDC where your account already is.

Because the account is permanent and reusable, every future deposit is simply another transfer to an account you already hold. Set it up once, and the rail stays open. US dollars arrive one-to-one, and Infinex doesn't add a fee.

## What to expect on timing

Once your bank releases the transfer, it settles on its own schedule. ACH and wires usually arrive within a day; a wire can take up to a couple of days. Your deposit moves through clear states, and the page updates as it goes.

## What it doesn't do yet

Plainly, so there are no surprises:

- **Deposits only.** You can bring dollars in. Sending dollars back out to a bank (off-ramp) isn't part of this release.
- **US dollars to start.** EUR bank transfers (SEPA) are coming soon. Other currencies will follow.
- **Your main Infinex account.** Deposits land in your main account, not vaults or imported wallets.
- **Where Bridge can operate.** Some regions are restricted by Bridge's own compliance rules.

## What your money can do once it's here

Once your funds are USDC on Base, they're ready to move across Infinex. Trade perps on Hyperliquid. Swidge into almost any asset on almost any chain. Put your stablecoins to work for yield. Take a position on Polymarket.

And Hyperliquid spot is coming in the weeks ahead, so what you bring in today will have somewhere new to go soon.

Your bank account and your wallet used to be two separate things. Now they're one. The wall is on older maps.
