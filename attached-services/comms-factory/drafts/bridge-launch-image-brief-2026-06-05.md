# Bridge.xyz launch — image brief (designer request)

Not generated by us — a request for the designer. comms-factory does NOT specify visual treatment (palette / type / motion / illustration come from brand-factory's locked Infinex spec). This brief carries **intent + content + guardrails**; the *look* is the designer's, within the brand spec.

## Where it's used
- Primary image for the **X post** (single).
- Lead image for **tweet 1 of the X thread** (can reuse the primary, or a variant).
- Optional: one supporting image for a later tweet in the thread.

## What it has to communicate (the message, not the styling)
Your bank account and your on-chain wallet used to be two separate places — now they're one. You can fund Infinex straight from your bank, and it arrives as USDC on Base. Calm, matter-of-fact, "the wall is gone" — not a hype moment.

## Content cues (suggestions, not prescriptions — designer's discretion)
- The bank → Infinex-wallet connection: two things becoming one account / a removed boundary.
- USD in → USDC on Base out.
- Optional anchor text (keep minimal; the copy lives in the post, not the image): e.g. "Fund Infinex from your bank" or "USDC on Base." Prefer little-to-no on-image text.

## Format
- X-optimized: 16:9 (1600×900) for the post card; a 1:1 variant is handy too if easy.

## Guardrails (hard)
- **No hype / urgency visuals** — no "act now," countdowns, explosions, rockets. Infinex is Spell→Vision, not Passion. Calm confidence (banker-turned-crypto-trailblazer).
- **No AI-slop aesthetics** — no generic purple gradients, no cliché crypto-glow.
- **No competitor palettes or logos** (Polymarket / Pendle / Phantom / Monad / Hyperliquid / Berachain hues are off-limits).
- Treatment per the **locked Infinex brand spec** — palette, type, motion are brand-factory's call, not ours.

## Deliverable
1 primary launch image (16:9, + optional 1:1). Optional 1 supporting image for the thread.
